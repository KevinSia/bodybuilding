### Warming up

- Warm up: loosen muscles
- Stretching: increase flexibility
- Examples of warm up:
   - Runner: light jog.
   - Martial arts: few light kicks and punches. 
   - Bodyweight workouts: basic body rotations. 
   - Lift weight: do a few lifts with very light weights.
